Denton High School

Mission Statement

Denton High School, in partnership with the home and community is dedicated to the development

of knowledgeable, compassionate individuals who actively and ethically contribute to the

betterment of our world.

By providing challenging and educational experiences that encourage cooperation, creativity and

intercultural understanding, DHS aims to create life-long learners who embrace the diversity of

others.
